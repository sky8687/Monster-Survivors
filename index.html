About Monster Survivors

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an engaging survival game that puts your strategic thinking and quick reflexes to the test. As a lone survivor, you must navigate through increasingly challenging waves of monsters while upgrading your abilities and equipment.

Key Features

  • Dynamic wave-based gameplay
  • Character progression system
  • Diverse monster types
  • Strategic upgrade paths
  • Engaging combat mechanics

How to Play

  1. Start the game and choose your initial character
  2. Survive waves of monsters by using your basic attacks
  3. Collect experience points to level up
  4. Choose upgrades to enhance your abilities
  5. Progress through increasingly challenging waves
  6. Try to achieve the highest survival time possible
